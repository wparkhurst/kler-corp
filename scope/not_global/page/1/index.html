Fully Integrated ERP System

Simplify Your Work Life With A SaaS Business System

KLER has integrated business best practices including manufacturing, as well as procurement, sales and staff management, to produce a state of the art ERP platform. We have solved the largest pain points of competing systems making sure we provide a true Seed-to-Sale ERP system to a market in need.

Cultivation

Manage the workflow of your seeds from planting to harvest to sales including expense tracking, inspections, movement tracking and so much more.

Production

Easily create products using raw materials. Turn flower to pre-rolls, concentrates, and edibles using the easy to manage templates and recipes.

Inventory

Keep your inventory visible and maintain accountability throughout your business operations.  Every change to inventory tracks the employees involved, transaction history, originating batch, and date/time of the change.

Wholesale

Control sales, prepare distribution and manage transportation of your organization’s inventory.

Purchasing

KLER allows you to take control of your purchasing.  Automatic reordering, unit of measure conversions, and holds your vendors accountable with order versus received product tracking.

Logistics

Complete visibility into operations allows effective management of the product lifecycle.

Retail

Purpose-built POS system combined with world-class interface design provides a smooth, effortless sales process.

Admin - HR

Manage your biggest expense with KLER.  Track employee costs, hours worked, sales per hour, and maintain true employee accountability from optimizing schedules to production efficiency.

Analytics

Custom analytics based on your business to build budgets, create forecasts and manage projects.
